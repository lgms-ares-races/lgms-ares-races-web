# Hospital Net

**Monthly SCCo Hospital Net**

On the fourth Wednesday of each month at 7:00p, the 11 Hospitals that form the Santa Clara County Hospital Net practice using the Hospital Net Emergency Radio station at each of the participating hospitals. El Camino Hospital - Los Gatos is the designated public emergency hospital for Los Gatos-Monte Sereno. Both voice and packet data check-ins are performed. The November Hospital Net is a StateWide Medical and Health Exercise (SWMHE) held the morning of the Thursday before Thanksgiving. There is no Hospital Net in December.

Our LG/MS Emergency Communications group operates the Emergency Radio station for Hospital Net drills and in actual emergencies. Los Gatos/Monte Sereno Net Control for Hospital Net for the May check-in (4th Wednesday) each year.
